About Sudoku Classic

Sudoku Classic brings the timeless logic puzzle to your iPhone with a beautiful, distraction-free experience. Whether you're a beginner learning the ropes or an expert seeking a challenge, our 50 levels across four difficulty tiers have something for everyone.

Key Features

  • 50 Levels across Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert difficulties
  • Progressive difficulty — levels get harder as you advance within each tier
  • Undo & Redo — never worry about mistakes
  • Note mode — pencil in candidates to solve tougher puzzles
  • Smart error detection — know instantly when you've gone wrong
  • Star ratings — earn up to 3 stars per level
  • Sound effects & haptics — satisfying audio feedback
  • No internet required — play completely offline
  • No ads, no in-app purchases — a clean, premium experience
